WHAT ARE WE LOOKING FOR?

In all these roles we are looking for motivated individuals who thrive in a team environment and are eager to take on real engineering challenges. You don't need prior experience in rocketry, what matters most is a willingness to learn, contribute, and grow alongside the team. Strong communication, problem-solving ability, and practical skills are highly valued, as is the drive to collaborate and share knowledge across different subsystems. If you are passionate about developing new skills and working on ambitious hands-on projects, we'd love to hear from you.
